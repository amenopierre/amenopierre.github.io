Welcome!

I am a postdoc in the Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics department (APAM) of Columbia University. My advisor is Michael Weinstein.

In December 2023, I defended the PhD thesis titled Wave propagation in quasiperiodic media, under the supervision of Sonia Fliss and Patrick Joly.

I am interested in the analysis and the numerical simulation of wave propagation phenomena in presence of periodic or quasiperiodic media.
